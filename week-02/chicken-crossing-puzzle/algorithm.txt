CHICKEN-FOX-CORN RIVER CROSSING PUZZLE

Problem:
A farmer transports chicken, fox, and corn across a river.
Boat holds farmer + one item.
Unsafe: chicken+fox alone OR chicken+corn alone.

Algorithm:
1. Initialize: river_left = ["chicken", "fox", "corn"], river_right = []
2. Safety check: Convert list to sorted tuple, check against unsafe pairs
3. Solution steps:
   - Take chicken right
   - Return alone
   - Take fox right
   - Bring chicken back (KEY STEP)
   - Take corn right
   - Return alone
   - Take chicken right

Complexity: O(1) time, O(1) space
